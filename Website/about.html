About Tiger Radio

More Music Than Anyone Else.

WQTR-FM is an actual FM radio station broadcasting on 106.1 FM from the campus of Hardin County High School. It's run entirely by the students of Hardin County High School's three broadcasting classes and overseen by Mr. Ron Shank, a broadcasting veteran, previously co-host of the JJ and Ron in the Morning Show and Program Director at American Family Radio, a national radio network comprised of nearly 200 stations.

We are a “new and improved Tiger Radio.” We are now playing music that targets the parents and students of HCHS, increasing time spent listening in one of the most important listener demographics. Our variety of hits from the 80s, 90s through today makes it easy to listen to at work and throughout the day.

What Makes Us Different:

We are a non-profit educatioanl station and therefore, we are restricted by law from advertising. But like public radio and Christian radio, we are allowed to accept sponshorships, also known as underwriting. Federal Law prohibits us "calls to action" like, "Call NOW! Come on down! Hurry, Hurry, Hurry! Etc" But there are things we can do.

A Better Way

Many business owners think underwriting is a better way for your business to get the word out. Let’s face it, when you are in your car and you hear a commercial on the radio, you probably do like most people do and change the station. When you sponsor Tiger Radio, you are quickly labeled as a sponsor in a very unobtrusive way. We take fewer breaks and our listeners not only appreciate us for it, but they will also appreciate you as a sponsor.

Student Run

The biggest reason people listen is our content is student-run. Students read the news, report on weather and sports scores, conduct interviews, and it’s a student who will record and produce your underwriting announcement.

We Need You!

To be perfectly honest, we need you. To operate this program with excellence is expensive and we need sponsors, like you, to make it all it can be. Thank you again for your support.
